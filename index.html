About Me

Hello my name is William Homer and I'm currently learning to become a full stack JavaScript web developer.

I'm currently working towards my undergrad in computer programming online attending Champlain College based in Burlington VT.

I enjoy following larger companies to see new software or products that they are releasing or working on. A few of these companies are Apple, Amazon and Google (Alphabet).

I've spend the last eight years of my life torn between an operational lifestyle and IT projects. It appears that these are, at least for my current company, merging into one as sales is driving operations for a better product and service and that drives operations to demand applications from IT.

This is what drives me. To find the current way something is done, find the bottle knecks, address incomming sales and then work towards a solution through the use of modular applications

I've grown up north of Chicago and have lived in the surronding suburbs for the majority of life. I currently live in a small town called Island Lake with my dog Ace. My immediate family lives in the area with my sister and brother both living in the same town.
